The Dole Premium Cavendish Banana is naturally sweet and packed with essential nutrients. Each medium-sized banana (118g) provides about 105 calories, with 27g of carbohydrates, 3g of dietary fiber, and 1.3g of protein. They are an excellent source of vitamin B6, vitamin C, and potassium. Bananas are known for their convenience as a portable snack, energy booster, and versatility in recipes from smoothies to baking. These bananas are ethically sourced from farms in Mindanao, Philippines, and carefully ripened to ensure peak flavor and nutrition.

While shopping for fruits, be aware that appearance isn't always indicative of nutritional value. Many fruits are treated with ripening agents or waxed for appearance. Dole Cavendish Bananas have a higher nutritional density than many other fruits, with more potassium per serving than most competitors. For maximum nutritional benefit, consume when yellow with small brown spots appearing.
