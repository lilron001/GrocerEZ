Prime Select Free-Range Chicken Breast offers premium quality poultry raised in spacious, open environments with access to natural sunlight and fresh vegetation. The meat is processed in a HACCP-certified facility under strict temperature controls to ensure food safety. Prime Select's network of partner farms in Batangas follows stringent animal welfare standards, allowing birds to exhibit natural behaviors while protecting them from predators and extreme weather. The meat is vacuum-sealed immediately after processing to maintain freshness and is delivered through a continuous cold chain to ensure optimal quality and safety at purchase.

When selecting chicken products, be aware that terms like "natural" have minimal regulatory requirements and don't necessarily indicate higher welfare standards or better nutrition.
