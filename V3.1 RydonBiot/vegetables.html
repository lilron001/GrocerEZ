Baguio Fresh Organic Kale is a nutrient powerhouse grown in the cool highlands of Benguet. This leafy green vegetable is exceptionally rich in vitamins K, A, and C, with significant amounts of B vitamins, calcium, potassium, and iron. A 100g serving contains only 49 calories while providing 4.3g of protein and 3.6g of fiber. The kale is grown using organic farming practices without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. The cool mountain climate produces sweeter leaves with tender texture compared to lowland-grown varieties. Harvested at peak maturity and delivered within 24 hours to ensure maximum freshness and nutrient retention.

When shopping for leafy greens, be aware that appearance can be misleading. Many vegetables are treated with preservatives to maintain visual freshness but may have reduced nutritional value. Baguio Fresh Kale contains significantly more bioavailable nutrients than conventionally grown alternatives. For maximum nutritional benefit, consume within 3-4 days of purchase and store in a humid environment. Consider the full nutritional profile rather than just price when comparing vegetables.
